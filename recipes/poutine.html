Poutine

Description:

Poutine is a famous Canadian dish that originates from the province of Quebec. It was created during the 1950s and consists of french fries and cheese curds covered in gravy.

Ingredients:
  • Straight cut french fries
  • White cheese curds
  • Beef gravy
Steps:
  1. Bake the french fries in an oven
  2. Place the cheese curds on top of the french fries
  3. Heat up the gravy and cover the french fries and cheese curds
  4. Wait until the cheese curds have melted
